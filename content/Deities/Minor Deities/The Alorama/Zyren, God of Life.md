_Title_: The Breath of Beginnings  
_Domains_: Beginnings, Blossoming Souls  
_Creators_: Almariel and Galeon  
_Pantheon_: Alorama

![](https://i.imgur.com/gwZKkFV.png)

**Description**  
**Zyren**, the God of Life, embodies the essence of creation, the spark that breathes life into the world, and the growth of the soul from birth. Created by [[Almariel, Lady of Light]]’s light and [[Galeon, Aevari of Air]]’s winds of inspiration, Zyren is the deity who oversees the beginnings of all life and the nurturing of new souls. Known as **The Breath of Beginnings**, Zyren represents the energy and vitality that starts every journey, infusing the world with the very essence of being.

Zyren is depicted as a radiant figure, surrounded by a gentle luminescence that represents the life force. He wears robes of pure white and sky blue, flowing like the breeze over a field of blossoms. His eyes are filled with warmth and understanding, and his hair appears to move gently as though touched by an unseen wind. In his hands, he carries an orb of light, symbolizing the pure essence of life. He is often depicted with butterflies and blooming flowers, symbols of transformation and the growth of new souls.

---

**Followers**  
Zyren’s followers, comprising midwives, caretakers, [[Clerics]], healers, and those who cherish the nurturing of new beginnings. They maintain temples dedicated to Zyren that are often places of birth and refuge, filled with vibrant life. The Lifebearers celebrate the start of all things, honoring the spark of vitality in every new beginning.

Temples dedicated to Zyren are serene places where the focus is on life, love, and renewal. These temples are adorned with blooming flowers, flowing water, and symbols of rebirth, reflecting Zyren’s influence over life in all its forms.

---

**Rituals and Offerings**

1. **The First Breath**: This sacred ritual is performed whenever a new life is brought into the world. The **First Breath** ceremony involves lighting a candle in Zyren’s honor, symbolizing the spark of life that has entered the world. Prayers are spoken to thank Zyren for the new soul and to ask for his blessings over the child’s journey.
    
2. **The Blossoming Day**: Once each year, followers of Zyren celebrate **The Blossoming Day** to honor the growth of all living things. It is a day of planting, nurturing gardens, and releasing butterflies as symbols of transformation. Followers reflect on the beauty of life and offer prayers for new beginnings and continued growth.
    
3. **Offerings**: Offerings to Zyren include flowers, glowing orbs made of glass, and vessels of pure spring water. These are placed at his altars as symbols of appreciation for the vitality Zyren grants to all living beings.
    

---

**Sigils and Symbols**  
Zyren’s symbol is a **butterfly emerging from an orb of light**, representing the blossoming of life and the start of a soul’s journey. His followers wear this sigil as a charm or paint it on cradles and doorways to invite his blessing for new life. It is also often seen in places where new beginnings are cherished, such as nurseries and gardens, as a sign of Zyren’s nurturing presence.

**Additional Details**  
**The Lifetouched**, those particularly blessed by Zyren, are said to have a unique aura that soothes and nurtures those around them. Their presence is often calming, and they have a natural talent for healing and nurturing. The Lifetouched are respected within their communities, often serving as caretakers and midwives, helping others in their most vulnerable moments.

Zyren’s influence can be felt in the first breaths of a newborn, the gentle flutter of a butterfly, and the warmth of sunlight on a new spring day. He teaches that life is a sacred journey, that every beginning is a beautiful opportunity for growth, and that nurturing the souls around us is one of the most sacred acts.
