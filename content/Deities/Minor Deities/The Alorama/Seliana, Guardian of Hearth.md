_Title_: The Flame of Home  
_Domains_: Family, Community  
_Creators_: Almariel and Pyrael  
_Pantheon_: Alorama

![](https://i.imgur.com/GsHJYlR.png)

**Description**  
**Seliana**, the Guardian of Hearth, embodies the warmth and security of family and the strength of community. Born from [[Almariel, Lady of Light]]’s nurturing light and [[Pyrael, Aevari of Fire]]’s vibrant flame, Seliana represents the sacred bond that holds families together and the unyielding spirit of communal care. Known as **The Flame of Home**, she is worshipped as the protector of households, the keeper of warmth, and the strength behind every thriving village.

Seliana is depicted as a radiant figure whose presence exudes comfort and warmth. She wears flowing robes of deep red and orange, evoking the colors of a cozy hearth fire. Her hair, like gently flickering flames, glows in hues of red, while her eyes are filled with the kind, steadfast light of a watchful guardian. In her hands, she holds a lantern, symbolizing her role as the bringer of warmth and safety to all homes.

---

**Followers**  
**[[The Hearthwardens]]** are Seliana’s devoted followers, comprising homemakers, community leaders, and those who cherish the values of family and togetherness. They maintain her shrines, often built at the heart of villages, as a place for gathering and celebration. The Hearthwardens uphold traditions that strengthen bonds, protect the vulnerable, and foster communal spirit.

Communities devoted to Seliana often feature her imagery in their homes and public spaces, believing her blessing ensures safety, warmth, and a sense of belonging. Her temples are places of refuge, where the needy find comfort and where festivals that celebrate life, love, and togetherness are held.

---

**Rituals and Offerings**

1. **The Kindling of the Hearth**: This daily ritual is performed by families in honor of Seliana. Each evening, the hearth is kindled while a short prayer is spoken, asking for warmth, safety, and Seliana’s presence in the home. It is believed that the flame carries her blessing and protection through the night.
    
2. **The Festival of Gathering**: Held once a year, this festival brings entire communities together to celebrate the bonds that unite them. Feasts are prepared, and the lanterns of Seliana are lit to symbolize her blessing over the community. The **Festival of Gathering** serves to renew communal ties and foster unity.
    
3. **Offerings**: Offerings to Seliana include bundles of herbs (such as rosemary and sage) used for purifying homes, freshly baked bread symbolizing sustenance, and small handcrafted lanterns. These offerings are placed at her shrine to ensure the household’s continued warmth and prosperity.
    

---

**Sigils and Symbols**  
Seliana’s symbol is a **lantern framed by two outstretched hands**, representing the warmth and security of home and the protective embrace of community. Her followers wear this sigil as pendants or inscribe it above their hearths to invoke her blessing. It is also carved into the doorposts of homes and community halls, marking them as places of refuge and warmth.

**Additional Details**  
**The Hearthblessed**, those who are particularly favored by Seliana, are said to have a calming presence that can ease conflicts and bring warmth wherever they go. They are often called upon to mediate disputes, bless new homes, or lead community gatherings. These individuals embody Seliana’s values, spreading her influence through acts of kindness, hospitality, and fostering unity.

Seliana’s influence can be felt in every warm embrace, every shared meal, and every light that shines through the darkness of night. She teaches that true strength lies in the bonds between people, in the warmth of a shared fire, and in the love that makes a house a home.
