### Overview

Alvoryn, the Keeper of Dreams, is the goddess of prophecy, visions, and the mystic threads that weave into the fabric of fate. Formed through the combined power of [[Almariel, Lady of Light]], and [[Galeon, Aevari of Air]], Alvoryn is a figure of mystery who straddles the boundaries between the waking world and the [[Dreamscape]], a plane through which she communes with her followers and whispers fragments of what is to come. Known to bestow rare visions upon chosen seers, Alvoryn's blessings are both revered and feared, as her prophecies can bring either comfort or dread. Her followers believe her dreams reveal hidden truths, guidance for the lost, and glimpses into the threads of fate that only she can see.

---

### Followers

Alvoryn's followers are a dedicated and unique order known as [[The Veiled Seers]]. They are often mystics, oracles, and dreamwalkers who seek to interpret her visions and offer guidance to those who seek her wisdom. Her followers are typically solitary figures, known for their quiet, introspective lives. They often live in temples isolated in high places, such as cliffside sanctuaries or hidden mountain retreats, where they can be closer to the Dreamscape.

The Veiled Seers are not only respected for their connection to Alvoryn but are also considered sacred guides in the world of Eldara, often advising leaders in times of uncertainty or crisis.

---

### Rituals and Practices

1. **The Dreamfall Rite**: This sacred ritual is performed on the night of each new moon. The Veiled Seers drink a sacred draught made from herbs that allow them to slip into a deep, trance-like state. In this ritual, they enter the Dreamscape to commune with Alvoryn, seeking her guidance and bringing back messages that could foretell important events.
    
2. **The Silent Vigil**: Followers observe a period of silence each year on the day of the equinox, known as the Day of Shadows. During this time, the faithful dedicate themselves to quiet contemplation and meditation. It is believed that on this day, Alvoryn comes closest to the waking world and may whisper directly to those with open minds.
    
3. **Dream Offerings**: Alvoryn’s followers place offerings of silk-bound scrolls containing their dreams and hopes for the future in sacred pools or small, clear rivers. Each scroll is inscribed with symbols and anointed with oils, then gently set adrift as a humble request for Alvoryn’s blessing.
    

---

### Sigils and Symbols

Alvoryn’s sigil is a crescent moon entwined with delicate, spiraling vines that represent the ever-twisting threads of fate and dreams. Her followers often tattoo this sigil upon their hands or wrists, or wear it as a pendant over their hearts to signify their devotion. The symbol is also inscribed at the entrance of her temples to mark a sacred space for dreaming and prophecy.

### Offerings

Offerings to Alvoryn often include:

- **Silken Threads**: In honor of her role as the weaver of dreams, followers leave silk or thread as offerings, symbolizing the many pathways that connect fate and prophecy.
- **Crystals and Moonstones**: These represent clarity and insight, believed to enhance dreams and deepen one’s connection to the Dreamscape.
- **Sacred Waters**: Clear waters collected under the light of the full moon are left as offerings, symbolizing purity and the ability to see beyond the surface.

---

### Additional Details

It is said that those blessed or burdened by Alvoryn’s favor are marked with a unique sign: their eyes appear to change colors slightly during sleep, reflecting the realm of dreams they traverse. These individuals, known as _Dreammarked_, often possess a heightened sensitivity to Alvoryn’s influence and are considered her messengers among mortals. In return, they bear the weight of seeing visions that others cannot comprehend, a gift that binds them closer to the goddess yet separates them from ordinary life.

The Keeper of Dreams remains an enigmatic figure, whose power lies in whispers and the secrets carried by the wind. Alvoryn’s visions are as elusive as mist, providing guidance for those who know how to interpret her messages, and holding them to a path determined by destiny rather than desire.
