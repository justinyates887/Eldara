_Title_: The Blooming Dawn  
_Domains_: Rebirth, New Beginnings  
_Creators_: Almariel, Valora, Pyrael  
_Pantheon_: Alorama

![[Pasted image 20250417124743.png]]

**Description**  
**Viridia**, the God of Spring, embodies renewal, rebirth, and the joy that accompanies new beginnings. Created by [[Almariel, Lady of Light]]’s nurturing light, [[Valora, Aevari of Water]]’s life-giving waters, and [[Pyrael, Aevari of Fire]]’s spark of warmth, Viridia represents the convergence of elements that heralds the changing of seasons and the awakening of the world from winter’s hold. Known as **The Blooming Dawn**, Viridia is celebrated as the bringer of new life, the vibrant deity who breathes freshness into the world.

Viridia is depicted as a figure of great beauty and vitality, adorned in flowing robes of green, pink, and gold, evoking the colors of blossoming flowers and budding leaves. Her hair is crowned with flowers in full bloom, and her eyes are as bright as the early spring sun. In her hands, she carries a staff from which sprouts fresh blossoms and young leaves, representing the life that flourishes in her wake.

---

**Followers**  
**[[The Verdant Circle]]** are Viridia’s followers, comprising gardeners, [[druids]], and those who cherish the renewal of life. They maintain shrines and groves dedicated to her, celebrating the rejuvenation of the natural world. The Verdant Circle reveres Viridia as the symbol of hope and new opportunities, ensuring that her presence is felt in every growing bud and flowing stream.

Temples dedicated to Viridia are often found in gardens, groves, or meadows, where flowers and trees can flourish. These temples are places of beauty and reflection, welcoming those who seek renewal, both for the land and for their spirits.

---

**Rituals and Offerings**

1. **The Blooming Celebration**: At the beginning of spring, followers of Viridia gather for the **Blooming Celebration**. During this event, the faithful plant flowers and young saplings while singing hymns to honor the goddess of renewal. The festival is filled with dances, flower crowns, and offerings of fresh blooms, symbolizing the hope and beauty of spring.
    
2. **The Dawn Planting**: The **Dawn Planting** is a sacred ritual where followers rise at dawn to plant seeds, believing that the morning light and Viridia’s blessing will ensure their growth. This ritual represents new beginnings, not just for the crops but also for those taking part.
    
3. **Offerings**: Offerings to Viridia include baskets of spring flowers, vials of dew collected at dawn, and wreaths of fresh greenery. These are left at her altars as symbols of appreciation for the life and beauty she brings to the world.
    

---

**Sigils and Symbols**  
Viridia’s symbol is a **blooming flower encircled by radiant sunrays**, representing the renewal of life and the energy of new beginnings. Her followers often wear this sigil as a pendant, carve it onto garden stones, or weave it into floral wreaths to invoke her blessing. It is also used to decorate doorways and windows during springtime, symbolizing the hope and joy that Viridia’s presence brings.

**Additional Details**  
**The Springborn**, those particularly favored by Viridia, are said to possess an extraordinary affinity for plants. Gardens tended by the Springborn flourish with unmatched vibrancy, and their presence seems to encourage the growth of all living things. The Springborn are revered as healers and nurturers, often called upon to bless newly planted fields or aid in the recovery of withering lands.

Viridia’s influence is felt in every gentle breeze, every blossom breaking through the thawing earth, and every heart that finds the courage to start anew. She teaches that each ending brings a new beginning, and the cycle of life is filled with endless possibilities for growth and renewal.
