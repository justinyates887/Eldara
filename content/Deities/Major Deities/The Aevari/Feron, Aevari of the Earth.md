Known as the **Stone Father**, Feron embodies strength, endurance, and the deep mysteries of the earth. He is the patron of miners, craftsmen, and those who build. Ancient and unyielding, he’s often depicted as a towering figure with skin like granite, veins of precious metals, and eyes like glowing embers. Worshipers call on him for stability and resilience.

**Alignment:** Lawful. Mostly aligns with the wishes of Almariel. Believes in structure. 

**Feron’s Creations** – All beings of the earth, from dwarves and giants to beasts of the land, trees, and plants rooted in the soil.
