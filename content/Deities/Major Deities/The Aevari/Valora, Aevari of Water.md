**The Tidebringer**, as a spirit of water, change, and mystery, Valora controls the oceans and all rivers and is honored as a protector of sailors and a bringer of fortune—or misfortune. She’s both beautiful and fearsome, representing the ever-changing nature of water, with the calm of a lake but the fury of a tsunami.

**Valora’s Creations** – All aquatic beings, rivers, lakes, and creatures tied to the tides, like merfolk and oceanic beasts.
