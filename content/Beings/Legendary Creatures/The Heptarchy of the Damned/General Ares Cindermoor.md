#Spoilers

A former military commander with a barely contained volcanic temper. Ares presents himself as a strategic advisor but specializes in stoking conflicts between allies. He whispers of slights and injustices, turning minor disagreements into blood feuds. His rage burns cold and calculated. He doesn't explode, he orchestrates explosions in others.

![](https://i.imgur.com/jsNvq5Y.jpeg)

Physical Appearance: Bears a battle-scarred face with a prominent scar across one cheek, a reminder of conflicts past. His eyes burn like hot coals, smoldering with orange-red intensity that never fully dims. Close-cropped hair shows ash-gray streaks, as if the fires of rage have begun to burn through even his appearance. His broad shoulders and muscular build are barely contained by his clothing, and his jaw remains perpetually clenched with visible tension running through every muscle. When his anger rises, his veins seem to glow faintly beneath his skin, like embers ready to ignite into flame.
