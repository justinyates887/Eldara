#Spoilers

A stunning sorceress who believes herself superior to all others. Narcissa corrupts through flattery and false elevation, convincing rulers they are divine and above counsel. She feeds their egos until they become tyrants, isolated by their own arrogance and unable to see threats or make wise decisions.

![](https://i.imgur.com/cnlFYFr.png)

Physical: Possesses impossibly symmetrical features that seem almost too perfect, creating an uncanny valley beauty that both attracts and disturbs. Her hair falls like polished obsidian, never a strand out of place, while her mirror-bright violet eyes reflect more light than they should, as if capturing and amplifying every gaze that falls upon her. Her skin has an ethereal luminescence, as if lit from within by her own sense of superiority. She maintains unnaturally perfect posture, her spine always straight, and adorns herself in elaborate gowns that seem to shift and shimmer with every movement, demanding attention from everyone in her presence.
