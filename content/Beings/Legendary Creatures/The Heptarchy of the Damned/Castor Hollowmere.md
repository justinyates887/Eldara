#Spoilers #NPC

A corpulent nobleman with an insatiable appetite for all forms of excess. Castor appears as a jovial host who encourages indulgence in food, drink, and pleasure, but his true power lies in creating dependency. He corrupts through abundance, turning allies into addicts of luxury and comfort until they become weak, complacent, and easily controlled.

![](https://i.imgur.com/WZVLcKo.png)

Physical Appearance: Moves with surprising grace despite his corpulent frame, his flushed and jovial face always featuring perpetually rosy cheeks that suggest fine wine and rich meals. His small, bright eyes sparkle with mirth and invitation, while his soft hands are adorned with jeweled rings that catch the light. He dresses in the finest silks that strain slightly at the seams, a testament to his philosophy of abundance and indulgence. The scent of wine and rich foods clings to him like a second skin, announcing his presence before he enters a room.
