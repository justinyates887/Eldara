#Spoilers

A captivating enchantress who specializes in romantic manipulation. Scarlett doesn't seduce directly. Instead, she orchestrates affairs, unrequited loves, and romantic obsessions among the nobility. She destroys alliances through broken hearts and turns political marriages into scandalous betrayals.

![](https://i.imgur.com/IdFbCjA.png)

Physical Appearance: Carries herself with deliberate enchantment, her rose-petal lips permanently curved in a knowing smile that suggests she understands desires people haven't yet admitted to themselves. Her warm amber eyes seem to see through facades and into hearts, while her auburn hair falls in natural ringlets that frame her face with effortless romance. Delicate beauty marks are strategically placed across her features, and every gesture she makes is graceful and deliberate. She dresses in deep crimsons and romantic pastels that evoke passion and longing.
