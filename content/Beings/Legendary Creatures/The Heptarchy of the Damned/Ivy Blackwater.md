#Spoilers

An elegant courtier with poison-green eyes who excels at identifying what people desire most. Ivy appears helpful, offering to "assist" others in achieving their dreams, but instead breeds resentment and competition. She turns friends into rivals by highlighting inequalities and making everyone feel they deserve more than they have.

![](https://i.imgur.com/JeoaErj.png)

Physical Appearance: Studies the world through poison-green eyes that seem to catalog everything they see, measuring and comparing with ruthless precision. Her sharp, angular features give her an elegant severity that speaks to her calculating nature. She often pulls her dark hair back severely, emphasizing those evaluating eyes and the thin lips often pressed together in assessment. Her pale skin takes on a slight greenish tint in certain light, like verdigris on copper. She dresses impeccably in deep emeralds and blacks, her style always perfect and always highlighting what others lack.
