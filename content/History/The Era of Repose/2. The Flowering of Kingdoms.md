#Spoilers
_Years 501-1000_

**Summary:** The kingdoms mature and develop their distinctive identities. **[[Aelarion]]** becomes the multicultural heart of the world, where elven founders and diverse immigrants create a magnificent melting pot. **[[Kingdom of Vanguard Reach]]** develops its martial excellence, forges burning day and night, producing the finest warriors. **[[Kingdom of Lenthir]]** masters agricultural abundance, its great plains feeding the continent. **Caernast** builds honest trade networks connecting all kingdoms. **[[Khalgrod Deep]]'s** dwarven holds become centers of legendary craftsmanship. **[[The Greenfell]]** develops a peaceful, prosperous agrarian culture.

This is genuine prosperity. The arts flourish. Magical innovation advances rapidly in Aelarion and Verdant Reach's colleges. Cross-cultural exchange leads to new philosophies and technologies. The Second Era's devastation becomes distant history, remembered in books but not in living memory.

The [[Tribunal of Balance]] continues active oversight, but there's less to oversee, the system works. Divine powers find fulfillment in guidance rather than control. Champions are chosen thoughtfully and serve well.

**Key Developments:**

- All kingdoms reach cultural maturity with their distinct, healthy identities
- Aelarion established as world center and multicultural mecca
- Verdant Reach emerges as military and forge capital of humanity
- Lenthir's agricultural excellence begins feeding the continent
- Caernast becomes vital trade hub near Khalgrod
- Inter-kingdom cooperation reaches peak effectiveness
- Arts, sciences, and magic advance significantly
- Tribunal maintains active but increasingly routine oversight
- No major crises—the Covenant genuinely prevents problems
- Zoroth continues to watch and wait, beginning preliminary designs
