- _Synopsis_: Ancient seers predict the arrival of a new age when mortal and divine powers will reach equilibrium, ushering in an era of potential peace or destruction. Eldara’s inhabitants brace themselves, preparing for the uncertain future.

- _Key Events_: Rise of prophecy and mystics; lore of a Second Dawn spreads through stories, filling mortals with either hope or dread, as they await the shift into the next era.
