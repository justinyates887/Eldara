- _Synopsis_: Weary from endless strife, the Seraphs of Purpose gather to forge a protective pact for Eldara, creating a divine barrier to prevent the Dread Cabal from directly influencing mortals. The barrier, however, has unintended consequences.

- _Key Events_: Creation of the Eternal Watch, a divine pact that limits direct interference in Eldara; mortal champions of both light and dark become proxies for their deities’ wills.
