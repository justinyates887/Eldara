- _Synopsis_: An ancient event occurs when forces from both sides vie to alter a powerful ley line that runs through Eldara. This ley line, believed to connect all life, becomes a battleground as both sides attempt to bend it to their own will.

- _Key Events_: Convergence at the ley line, creation of the first powerful artifacts, some imbued with light and others with shadow; a cataclysmic explosion that scars the land, splitting it into light and dark territories.
