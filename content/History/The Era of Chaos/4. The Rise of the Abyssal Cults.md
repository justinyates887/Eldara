- _Synopsis_: Worshipers of Vorthar’s dark gods, particularly those of Malikar and Xylar, begin establishing cults that spread unrest. Driven underground, they construct secret societies and hidden places of worship, extending influence through fear and control.

- _Key Events_: Expansion of the Underdark as cultists delve beneath the surface; creation of cursed artifacts and forbidden spells; rise of cult leaders whose powers rival minor gods.
