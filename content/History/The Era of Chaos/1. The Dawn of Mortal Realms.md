- _Synopsis_: The Seraphs of Purpose and the Dread Cabal begin influencing the Material Plane (or "Eldara") through their creations. Almariel’s and Vorthar’s minor gods actively shape the regions of Eldara, each working to build environments that suit their followers and ideological purposes.

- _Key Events_: Establishment of sacred places for both good and evil; creation of elemental regions where the influence of the Seraphs and Dread Cabal is strongest.
