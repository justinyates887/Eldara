- _Synopsis_: Tensions erupt as divine beings take on physical avatars or send emissaries to the mortal plane, inspiring (and sometimes directly leading) their followers into skirmishes. Vorthar’s jealousy manifests in monstrous invasions, corrupting lands and forests created by the Seraphs.

- _Key Events_: First major conflicts between followers of the Seraphs and the Dread Cabal; introduction of corruption as a force that can twist regions into dark and perilous landscapes.
