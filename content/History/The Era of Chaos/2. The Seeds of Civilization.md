- _Synopsis_: Early sentient races like humans, elves, orcs, and giants begin to build primitive societies. Guided by divine visions and sometimes manipulated by Vorthar's whispers, they establish the first clans and tribes, each group carving a place in the chaotic world.

- _Key Events_: Rise of the first strongholds, forming of clans; early alliances and rivalries begin to shape societies based on allegiance to different gods.
