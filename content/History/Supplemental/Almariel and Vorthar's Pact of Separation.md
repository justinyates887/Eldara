## **The First Ethyri Pact**

_Woven at the End of The Creation, Before the First Age_

### **Context**

As the cosmos came into being, shaped by the love and opposition of **[[Almariel, Lady of Light]]** and **[[Vorthar, The Dark Weaver]]**, their four children, the **Aevari**, were born from the union of light and shadow. Together, this divine family crafted the elemental planes and the mortal realm of **[[Eldara, the Shimmering Veil]]**, a world meant to be a canvas for growth, balance, and wonder.

Yet even in those early moments, tension arose. Almariel sought to nurture and protect, while Vorthar longed to control and reshape. Their conflicting visions for Eldara began to ripple through the very fabric of creation. The Aevari, young and full of vision, were torn between their parents' whispers and intentions. Conflict began to rise even before time had meaning.

To prevent open war and protect the fragile new world, **Almariel and Vorthar forged an unbreakable pact**, binding themselves to the Weave and placing limits upon their own divine reach.

---

### **The First Terms of the Pact**

1. **No Divine Touch Upon Eldara**  
    The Ethyri shall not **directly act upon the Material Plane**. They may not shape terrain, bestow power, create new life, or manifest avatars within the realm of mortals. Eldara belongs to the Aevari.
    
2. **The Aevari Hold Dominion Over the Material Plane**  
    All creation, evolution, and stewardship of life on Eldara falls to the Aevari. The Ethyri may offer wisdom to their children in dreams or omens, but **never command** them.
    
3. **No Mortal May Bear the Direct Will of the Ethyri**  
    The Ethyri may not speak directly to any being born of Eldara. Their influence must be **interpreted**, not received. Divine visions may come through dreams, symbols, or the echoes of magic, but never as commands or voices.
