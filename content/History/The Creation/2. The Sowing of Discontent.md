To further guide her children in the art of creation, **[[Almariel, Lady of Light]]** brought forth a new realm; a place where physical beings could be shaped, nurtured, and given purpose. This realm was called **[[Eldara, the Shimmering Veil]]**, known to mortals as the **Material Plane**. Almariel demonstrated the secrets of physical form and life, her light filling Eldara with beauty and vitality.

Together with her children, she began to create. In each collaboration, Almariel poured her wisdom and compassion, inspiring the Aevari to shape creatures that would reflect their own nature:

- **[[Feron, Aevari of the Earth]]** and Almariel crafted **giant beasts** with an unbreakable bond to the land itself. Among them were noble creatures like **giant eagles** and **good-aligned dinosaurs**, imbued with a sense of loyalty and strength that mirrored Feron’s own enduring nature.

- **[[Galeon, Aevari of Air]]** and Almariel breathed life into the **first birds**: **hawks, doves, and majestic eagles**, each species a testament to freedom, grace, and agility, symbolizing Galeon’s love of the skies.

- **[[Pyrael, Aevari of Fire]]** created alongside his mother **phoenixes** and **metallic dragons**—creatures of fire and light with scales that shimmered like polished metal. These beings embodied Pyrael’s fiery spirit, a blend of power and beauty, and they served as guardians of warmth and transformation.

- **[[Valora, Aevari of Water]]**, working with Almariel, filled the oceans with creatures that would know both the tranquil depths and fierce currents. Together, they created **whales**, **dolphins**, and **sea serpents of noble alignment**, each a symbol of Valora’s grace and the vast mystery of the seas.


Yet, as Almariel’s creations took form, **[[Vorthar, The Dark Weaver]]** grew bitter and envious. He loathed the brightness she instilled in their children’s creations, the inherent goodness woven into each being. Vorthar’s jealousy festered, driving him to twist the art of creation to reflect his own dark nature.

Embracing his own children’s desires, Vorthar crafted new beings alongside them, deceiving their trust and channeling his malcontent into their forms:

- With **Feron**, he created the crawling terrors of the earth. **Giant spiders**, **basilisks**, and **serpents** emerged, their instincts laced with malice and their forms shadowed. These creatures would lurk in the dark, striking fear into all who wandered near.

- With **Galeon**, Vorthar conjured creatures that would taint the skies: **vampire bats**, **harpies**, and **vulture-like rocs** that thrived in the night and feasted upon the weak.

- With **Pyrael**, Vorthar poured his fury, creating **chromatic dragons**—wicked beings of fire and poison, each scale seething with a different deadly hue. Alongside these, he unleashed **fire elementals** and malevolent **salamanders**, spirits of flame that scorched the land.

- With **Valora**, Vorthar’s influence birthed horrors from the depths. **Krakens**, **abhorrent eels**, and **aquatic aberrations** took form, their dark souls reflecting the chaos of the abyss, a reflection of Vorthar’s own hatred and envy.


Through these creations, the influence of light and shadow became a constant force in Eldara. The creatures of Almariel and Vorthar would forever coexist in a world now caught between the Ethyri’s creation and corruption, shaping a realm of beauty and danger.
