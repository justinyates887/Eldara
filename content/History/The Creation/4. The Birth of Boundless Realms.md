In the ages that followed, the Aevari brought forth diverse and intelligent life upon [[Eldara, the Shimmering Veil]]’s Material Plane, imbuing the world with beings that reflected their individual natures and dreams. Each Aevari crafted distinct forms of life with care and creativity, guiding their creations to grow and flourish across mountains, forests, skies, and seas.

Yet, as life blossomed, [[Vorthar, The Dark Weaver]] watched from the shadows with envy. Though he had agreed to relinquish his influence over the Material Plane, his ambition could not be fully contained. Determined to forge his own path through the unseen corners of Eldara, he drew upon the energies of darkness and hatred within himself. Vorthar cast his will into the depths, and there, he carved a plane of his own—a twisted reflection of creation, one that mirrored his bitterness and resentment. Thus, [[Udugmar]] came into being, a realm of shifting darkness and malice that would forever lurk beneath the visible world.

In this new plane, Vorthar fashioned a deity of his own: [[Zoroth, the Hollow Prince]]. This minor god, lesser than the Aevari, but cunning and relentless, was bound to the Abyssal Plane and granted dominion over its blackened depths. Vorthar whispered to Zoroth of the Material Plane, of the light-filled world above that he could never touch. Fueled by his father’s bitterness, Zoroth forged a connection between the Abyss and the mortal realm, crafting secret passageways and dark rivers that wove through stone and shadow. Thus, [[The Underdark]] was born; a labyrinthine domain beneath the land, accessible only through hidden chasms and ancient caverns, serving as the bridge between the Material Plane and the Abyss.

Witnessing Vorthar’s creation, [[Almariel, Lady of Light]] felt a pang of sorrow but also a resolve to counterbalance his darkness. She shaped a new plane of existence, one teeming with life and magic, a sanctuary where light and beauty could thrive undisturbed. She named it [[Liralor, the Feywild]]—a realm of ethereal wonder and perpetual wilds, where time moved differently and magic flowed freely. Liralor became home to radiant beings of pure joy and wisdom, sprites, and powerful Fey who guarded the beauty and purity of nature. Here, Almariel’s essence lived on, a world of dreams and enchantment that would inspire and protect the natural world from Vorthar’s encroaching shadows.

With the creation of the Abyss and Liralor, the universe was set into a delicate balance. The Material Plane now lay between these two realms, tethered to both the light of the Feywild and the darkness of the Abyss. The Aevari, unaware of the full depth of their parents’ conflict, continued to shape and tend to the world, their creations growing ever more diverse and wondrous. But beneath the surface, the machinations of Zoroth and the subtle whispers of Almariel and Vorthar ensured that the balance of light and darkness, life and death, would forever be in motion, as the intelligent beings they had crafted began to chart their own destinies across the vast world of Eldara.
