The Aevari stand in awe of the intricate life they have crafted, from the vast forests and towering mountains to the creatures that roam and populate these lands. They delight in the intelligence of their creations, but over time, they realize something is missing—a sense of purpose to guide and enrich the lives of these beings.

Turning to [[Almariel, Lady of Light]] and wisdom, the Aevari beseech her to breathe meaning into the lives of their creations. Hearing their plea, Almariel crafts a new class of deities—minor gods, each imbued with unique roles to inspire and shepherd the world's inhabitants.

These new gods are crafted to guide through every facet of existence, each with a domain that connects deeply to the mortal experience. As these minor gods emerge, each is tasked with crafting and sustaining their own domain within [[Eldara, the Shimmering Veil]].

Through their creation and care, these minor gods bring vibrancy and order, each corner of the world reflecting the essence of their domains. Their guidance ensures that life not only exists but flourishes, allowing mortals to live with purpose, bound to the will of their gods and to the world crafted for them by the Aevari. This intricate weave of responsibilities transforms the world into a rich tapestry, where each sector thrives under the watchful eyes of its guardian deity, echoing the harmony first envisioned by Almariel and the Aevari.

Witnessing the flourishing order and purpose bestowed upon the world by Almariel and her minor gods, [[Vorthar, The Dark Weaver]] seethes with resentment and jealousy. His own creations—shadowed beasts and dark creatures lurking in [[The Underdark]]—pale in comparison to the beauty and harmony of the Aevari's world. Determined to undermine Almariel’s influence and warp the balance established by her and the Aevari, Vorthar calls upon [[Zoroth, the Hollow Prince]], the deity he created to rule over [[Udugmar]], and they begin to conjure a new pantheon of minor deities. These dark gods are conceived to corrupt the virtues Almariel’s gods represent, sowing fear, hatred, chaos, and darkness in the hearts of mortals.

Vorthar’s malevolent deities mirror and mock the gods of purpose, planting seeds of discord that subtly infiltrate all sectors of mortal life. Each dark god stands opposed to an aspect of Almariel's world, aiming to twist it for Vorthar’s ambition.

Through these malevolent deities, Vorthar and Zoroth channel their dark influence into the world. Mortals, once drawn to the comforting light of Almariel’s purpose, now find themselves tempted by darker forces. The seeds of darkness begin to take root, casting shadows over the light that the Aevari worked so hard to cultivate.
