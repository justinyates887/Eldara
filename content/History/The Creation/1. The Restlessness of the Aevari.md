As eons passed, the **Aevari; [[Feron, Aevari of the Earth]], [[Pyrael, Aevari of Fire]], [[Galeon, Aevari of Air]],** and **[[Valora, Aevari of Water]]**—grew restless within the void. Their spirits yearned for more than the formless existence of energy and thought, feeling an untapped potential burning within them. Their dissatisfaction began to disturb the harmony of the Ethyri, and [[Almariel, Lady of Light]] and [[Vorthar, The Dark Weaver]] sensed their children’s need for purpose.

In response, the Ethyri joined their powers, creating a vast and beautiful universe from their own essence. Almariel’s light scattered across the void, giving rise to stars and galaxies, each a spark of warmth and brilliance. Vorthar wove his darkness through the cosmos, creating the emptiness between, the spaces where light dared not tread. This balanced universe, filled with equal parts light and darkness, became a canvas for their children’s ambitions.

As a final gift to their children, the Ethyri formed the **Elemental Planes**, realms aligned to each Aevari’s nature:

- **[[The Stonehold]]** for **Feron**: a domain of towering mountains, endless caves, and fertile valleys, where the earth itself responded to his will.
- **[[The Everflame]]** for **Pyrael**: an expanse of ceaseless flame and molten rivers, where fire roared as both destroyer and creator.
- **[[The Skyrealm]]** for **Galeon**: a boundless sky of endless winds and storms, where the air moved with freedom and whimsy.
- **[[The Abyssal Sea]]** for **Valora**: a realm of unfathomable depths and restless waves, where water flowed in endless cycles of calm and fury.

In addition, the Ethyri crafted the **[[Astral Plane]]**: a place between realms where the Aevari could experiment with creation itself and learn to shape the physical world. They challenged their children to enter this new physical existence, to discover and forge their identities, learning to create as their parents had.

With these realms established, the Aevari entered their own domains, each brimming with possibilities. This was the beginning of the Aevari’s journey toward shaping life and testing their powers, a time when creation itself was raw and boundless.
