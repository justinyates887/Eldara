_Years 201-250_

**[[The Alorama]]** and **[[The Nyx]]** begin choosing individual mortals for enhanced roles. The first primitive **Champions**. **Paladin Marcus Lightbringer** of **[[Belron, Defender of the Just]]** manifests abilities far beyond normal paladins. **Warmaster Theron Ironwill** begins experiencing visions from **[[Goroth, Lord of Bloodlust]]** that make him an unnaturally effective military commander. These early champions don't realize they're part of competing divine experiments in leadership.
