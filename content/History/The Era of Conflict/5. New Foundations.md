_Years 161-200_

Major reconstruction projects begin as survivors move beyond mere survival to building lasting settlements. Two philosophical approaches emerge: **[[Aelarion]]'s** model emphasizes peaceful cooperation and gradual healing, while other settlements focus on defensive strength and military readiness. The ruins of the **[[Kingdom of Vanguard Reach]]** attract survivors who believe the previous wars happened because humanity wasn't strong enough to defend itself. The ideological split that will define the era begins.
