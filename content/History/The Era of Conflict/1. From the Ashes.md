_Years 1-40_

Scattered human survivors emerge from hiding to find a continent scarred by magical devastation. **Queen Elara's** **[[The Kingdom of Aelarion]]** sends out expeditions to locate survivors and assess the scope of destruction. First contact with other remnant groups reveals that perhaps 5% of humanity survived the **Lost Generations**. Early settlements focus on basic survival: clean water, shelter, and avoiding the **Blighted Zones** where magical warfare poisoned the earth.
