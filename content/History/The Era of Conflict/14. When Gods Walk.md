_Years 651-700_

Divine avatars begin manifesting on the Material Plane as competition reaches unprecedented intensity. **[[Belron, Defender of the Just]]'s** avatar leads Alorama forces in the **Battle of Sacred Valleys**. **[[Goroth, Lord of Bloodlust]]'s** avatar responds by manifesting during the **Siege of Iron Gates**. The presence of actual deities on the battlefield causes reality distortions that persist for decades. Mortal soldiers report temporal loops, gravity anomalies, and attacks that wound the soul directly.
