_Years 851-900_

Initial negotiations begin between **[[The Alorama]]**, **[[The Nyx]]**, and mortal representatives. **The Assembly of Mortal Realms** forms to represent human interests in cosmic negotiations. Early talks focus on immediate ceasefires and reality stabilization. **The Neutral Grounds of [[Aelarion]]** become the meeting place where divine representatives can interact without triggering reality distortions through their conflicting energies.
