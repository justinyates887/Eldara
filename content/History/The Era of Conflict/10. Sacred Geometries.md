_Years 401-500_

Reality begins showing stress from intensifying divine influence. **[[The Aetheric Weave (Magic)]]** develops "knots" where conflicting divine energies interact. Areas around [[Aelarion (Region)]] experience chronically accelerated plant growth and unnaturally peaceful weather. Regions near the [[Kingdom of Verdant Reach]] see increased monster activity and spontaneous appearance of defensive terrain features. Scholars document that magic itself is becoming polarized along theological lines.
