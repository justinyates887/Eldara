_Years 41-80_

**[[The Kingdom of Aelarion]]** becomes the beacon of hope for human survivors, with **Queen Elara** (now ancient) establishing **Sanctuary Cities** for refugees. The kingdom develops new technologies for purifying contaminated land and water. **Elara's** philosophy of "healing through understanding" shapes reconstruction efforts, emphasizing cooperation and knowledge-sharing over territorial control. The first **Council of Remnants** convenes to coordinate continent wide recovery.
