_Years 351-400_

Competing divine messengers spread conflicting truths about humanity's proper direction. **Prophet Sarah Goldentongue** preaches [[Aelarion]]'s message of peace and healing across the continent. **Prophet Marcus Steelvoice** proclaims the  [[Kingdom of Vanguard Reach]]'s doctrine of strength through adversity. Their followers clash in "theological debates" that increasingly resemble military skirmishes. Other settlements are forced to choose sides or risk being seen as spiritually uncommitted.
