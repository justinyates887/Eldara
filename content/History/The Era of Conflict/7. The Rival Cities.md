_Years 251-300_

**The Kingdom of [[Aelarion (Region)]]** and the **Mortal [[Kingdom of Verdant Reach]]** emerge as rival models for humanity's future. Aelarion, influenced by **[[The Alorama]]**, focuses on peace, balance, and prosperity through cooperation and healing. Verdant Reach, guided by **[[The Nyx]]**, builds massive military forces and fortress-cities, believing that strength and preparation are the only defenses against future catastrophe. Both cities grow rapidly as survivors choose sides.
