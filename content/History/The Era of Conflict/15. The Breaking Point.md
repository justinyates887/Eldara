_Years 701-800_

Reality itself becomes unstable from excessive divine intervention. **Cascade failures** in **[[The Aetheric Weave (Magic)]]** create regions where magic functions unpredictably or not at all. Time flows differently in areas of intense divine conflict. **The Great Sundering** occurs when competing divine influences literally tear apart sections of landscape, creating the **Shattered Lands** where geography shifts daily based on theological allegiances.
