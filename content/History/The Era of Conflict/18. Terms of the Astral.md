_Years 901-1000_

The cosmic constitutional convention begins in earnest. **Divine philosophers**, **mortal legal scholars**, [[The Alorama]], and [[The Nyx]] work to draft comprehensive rules governing divine-mortal interaction. Major debates include the **Champion Limitation Principle**, **Divine Jurisdiction Boundaries**, and **Mortal Rights Protection**. The **Tribunal of Balance** structure emerges as a compromise between divine authority and mortal self-determination.
